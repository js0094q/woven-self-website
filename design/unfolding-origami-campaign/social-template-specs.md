# Social Template Specs

## Sizes

| Format | Size |
| --- | --- |
| Instagram feed post | `1080 x 1080` |
| Instagram story | `1080 x 1920` |

## Design Rules

- Use the Quiet Paper System.
- Use warm paper backgrounds, soft crease lines, folded-corner accents, and restrained origami motifs.
- Keep one clear message per asset.
- Keep typography editable and readable.
- Use Playfair Display for title or quote emphasis.
- Use Inter for labels, metadata, CTA text, and footer copy.
- Use `wovenself.com/author` as the visible URL.
- Keep preorder assets clear and conversion-focused.
- Keep quote and community assets quiet, spacious, and non-sensationalized.
- Do not use unsupported awards, bestseller claims, cure promises, clinical treatment guarantees, or outdated preorder-status language.
