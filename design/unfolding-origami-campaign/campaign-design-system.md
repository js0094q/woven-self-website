## Quiet Paper System

The Quiet Paper System is the campaign's reusable visual language. It should feel literary, restrained, warm, trauma-informed, clean, and quietly powerful.

Use it for:

- Social graphics.
- Print sell sheets and table tents.
- Optional author-page campaign components.
- Future preorder or launch assets.

## Motifs To Use

- Paper texture.
- Soft crease lines.
- Folded-corner details.
- Soft paper cards.
- Restrained origami line art.
- Generous whitespace.
- Warm cream and white surfaces.
- Sage and ink typography.
- Clay accents.

## Avoid

- Red and black danger palettes.
- Sensational trauma-marketing visuals.
- Stock therapy cliches.
- Overcrowded layouts.
- Heavy effects.
- Excessive gradients.
- Rage-bait visual language.
- Too many fonts.
- Unsupported awards or bestseller claims.
- Cure promises, clinical treatment guarantees, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Outdated preorder-status language.

## Campaign Palette

Use the CSS variables from `styles-campaign.css`:

```css
:root {
  --campaign-cream: #FBF7F0;
  --campaign-warm-white: #FFFDF9;
  --campaign-sage: #4A5C5A;
  --campaign-muted-sage: #7D8C88;
  --campaign-clay: #B98F76;
  --campaign-soft-clay: #E6D1C3;
  --campaign-ink: #2F3A38;
  --campaign-mist: #EDE8E1;
  --campaign-border: #D8CEC2;
}
```

Color usage:

- `--campaign-cream`: page or canvas background.
- `--campaign-warm-white`: paper-card surfaces.
- `--campaign-sage`: primary titles, buttons, and grounded accents.
- `--campaign-muted-sage`: secondary metadata and quiet support text.
- `--campaign-clay`: labels, small accents, and preorder detail emphasis.
- `--campaign-soft-clay`: folded-corner details.
- `--campaign-ink`: body copy and high-contrast text.
- `--campaign-mist`: soft dividers and secondary surfaces.
- `--campaign-border`: paper-card borders and QR placeholder outlines.

## Typography

- Headings, book-title emphasis, and pull quotes: Playfair Display.
- Body copy, labels, metadata, CTA text, and footer details: Inter.
- Keep type hierarchy simple: one title, one body block, one CTA or footer line per small social asset.
- Avoid dense blocks of copy on Instagram assets.

## Campaign Positioning

Primary positioning:

> A trauma therapist's memoir about surviving trauma, re-defining your shape, and folding back to yourself.

Primary hook:

> Some wounds don't bleed - they fold you inward.

Community line:

> For the ones who survived quietly.

Use reader-facing language that recognizes quiet survival without making clinical promises.
