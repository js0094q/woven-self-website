ABOUT THE BOOK

In this compelling narrative, the reader is guided by the author through her story of being a college freshman who becomes entangled in a coercive control, cult-like dynamic that ultimately culminates in the author being sexually assaulted.

This memoir reveals how easily someone can lose touch with their intuition when surrounded by gaslighting and manipulation. It’s a story of reclaiming self-trust, recognizing coercion in unexpected places, and the long, nonlinear journey of healing.
