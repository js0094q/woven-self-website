I may be the right therapist for you if…

  • You’ve talked about it for years, but your body never moved past it.
  • You feel emotionally reactive even when logically safe.
  • Your nervous system feels stuck on high alert.
  • You repeat relationship patterns you don’t understand.
  • Shame tells you that you “should be over it.”
  • Sleep is disrupted or intrusive memories show up unexpectedly.

If you’re nodding along, this isn’t failure — it’s how trauma lives in the nervous system.
