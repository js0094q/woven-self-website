I may be the right therapist for you if…

You’ve spent years talking about it, whether it’s the relationship, the incident, the abuse, but your body and brain never quite moved past it.

You might recognize yourself in some of the following:

  • Do you find yourself emotionally reactive even when you logically know you’re safe or “should be fine”?
  • Does your nervous system feel stuck on high alert, hypervigilant, shut down, or swinging between the two?
  • Do you notice repeating relationship patterns that don’t make sense to you, even though you’re self-aware?
  • Do you carry shame or self-criticism for not being “over it yet”?
  • Do your emotions feel intense or overwhelming, making it hard to focus on daily tasks?
  • Are your sleep patterns disrupted, trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or frequent nightmares?
  • Do intrusive or repetitive thoughts about past events show up when you least expect them?
  • Does your body feel tense, braced, or unable to fully relax, even during calm moments?

If you’re nodding along, this isn’t a personal failure, it’s how trauma lives in the nervous system.

Meet Loren Galese

Loren is a Licensed Professional Counselor in New Jersey (practicing since 2014). Her approach is person-centered and collaborative, with a focus on trust, pacing, and nervous system safety.

She is trained in EMDR and Internal Family Systems (IFS), and incorporates the Gottman Method and EFT for couples.
