Meet Lauren Rivers, LPC

With over 12 years of experience guiding clients through complex trauma, burnout, and relationship challenges, Lauren brings warmth, humor, and structure into each session. She is a Licensed Professional Counselor in New Jersey, an Approved Clinical Supervisor, and a lifelong student of mindful, culturally responsive care.

  • ✓Internal Family Systems (IFS) and polyvagal-informed techniques to restore inner trust.

Frequently asked questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. Alma verifies out-of-network benefits for most major plans, including Aetna, Cigna, Oxford, and UnitedHealthcare. You’ll see your estimated copay before confirming an appointment.

Can we meet virtually?

Absolutely. Telehealth sessions are available for anyone located in New Jersey. The video link is delivered via the secure Alma portal.

What is your cancellation policy?

Sessions can be canceled or rescheduled through Alma with 48 hours’ notice. Late cancellations are billed at the full session fee so we can honor everyone’s time.

Do you offer sliding scale spots?

A limited number of reduced-fee telehealth sessions are available each season. Join the waitlist through the contact form and I’ll reach out as openings arise.

Ready to begin?

Share a few details and our team will respond within two business days. For urgent mental health support, call or text 988 or visit your nearest emergency room.
