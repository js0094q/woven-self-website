About Loren Galese, LPC

Loren is the owner of The Woven Self Therapeutic Services, LLC and a Licensed Professional Counselor in New Jersey.

She brings together EMDR, Internal Family Systems, and trauma-informed relationship work in individual and couple sessions grounded in nervous system safety and collaborative pacing.

Her clinical writing and reflective memoir work come from the same direction: clarity, care, and practical containment.

For many clients, care begins with language that does not pathologize distress and with practical steps that regulate body and breath before changing behavior.
