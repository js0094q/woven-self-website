Hi! I’m Loren.

I’m Loren, the owner of Woven Self Therapeutic Services, LLC. I am a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of New Jersey who has been practicing since 2014.

My work centers on individuals, couples, and families navigating anxiety, trauma, relationship challenges, life transitions, stress, family issues, boundaries, and empowerment.

I am trained in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) and Internal Family Systems (IFS). For couples work, I incorporate the Gottman Method and Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT).
