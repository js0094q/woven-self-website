The Memoir

About the Memoir

Unfolding Origami follows a college freshman who becomes entangled in a coercive, cult-like dynamic that ultimately culminates in sexual assault.

The story traces how gaslighting, manipulation, and institutional failure can separate someone from their intuition, their voice, and their sense of safety.

It is also a memoir of return: reclaiming self-trust, recognizing coercion in unexpected places, and moving through the long, nonlinear work of healing.

Healing is not a straight line. It is a practice of unfolding, one careful crease at a time.
