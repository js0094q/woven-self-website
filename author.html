About the Author

Loren Galese is an emerging author whose work explores healing, emotional attunement, and the quiet internal narratives that shape who we become. Her writing blends therapeutic insight with accessible storytelling, helping readers reflect on their inner world with clarity and compassion.

Drawing from her therapeutic background in EMDR and Internal Family Systems, Loren's work focuses on the parts of ourselves that often go unheard, and the profound transformation that occurs when those parts are finally acknowledged.

Her forthcoming publications continue this thread, weaving together trauma-informed guidance with lyrical reflection.
